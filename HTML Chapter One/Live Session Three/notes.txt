Audio and Video Tag:
-------------------
preload is applicable on both audio and video tag.
it provide the hint to the browser that how much of the video content will be loded when the page load.

the attributes in preload are: 
    auto
    metadata
    none

preload = auto mean jo mera video hai it will be preloaded. jaise hi mera page load hoga
video apne aap chalega.

Some Important Points:
-------------------------
inline elements jo hamara hota hai unmein ham width nahi de sakhte but image mein toh ham de raha hai,
image ek inline elements hoke bhi ham us mein de rahe hein , img ek replaced inline element hai
jitne bhi replaced elements hote hein jaise ki input-tag , img-tag, text-tag , jitne bhi replaced 
elements hai unka khud ka kuch intrinsic dimension hota hein jaise ki ek image ek natural width aur height
hoti hai and they can accept width and height property even though they are inline.
